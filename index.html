Featured Recipe: Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp of oil or to your preference
  • 1/4 cup diced carrot
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 tbsp of diced garlic
  • 1/2 diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cups peas
  • 1/8 cup corn
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp of seasme oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

Step 1: Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. add carrot, onion, and garlic. Cook until onion is translucent. Step 2: Add bell peppers and broccoli. Cook for 3-5 minutes until tender. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan. Step 3: Pour beaten eggs into the empty side of the pan. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix them with the vegetables. Step 4: Add peas, corn, rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil salt and pepper. Mix well. Let rice cook till a little crispy. until heated through. Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy.
